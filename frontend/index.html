About Our Hostel Management System

Our platform is designed for educational institutions to manage hostel operations efficiently and securely. With a focus on data integrity, role-based access, and real-world workflows, HMS is trusted by admins and students alike.

  • Smart Room Management: Real-time allocation, occupancy tracking, and visual room displays.
  • Automated Fee Tracking: Streamlined fee management with reminders and payment status.
  • Complaint Resolution: Ticket-based system for quick grievance handling.
  • Leave & Mess Menu: Calendar-aligned leave view and day-wise mess menu cards.
  • Enterprise Security: JWT authentication, role-based middleware, and robust data modeling.
